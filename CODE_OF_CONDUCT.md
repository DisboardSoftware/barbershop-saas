# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical substance.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate disruption of collaborative work.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, block participation, or escalate actions depending on severity.
